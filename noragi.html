Noragi

Noragis (no-ra-gee) were a common farmer's garments. These jackets were patched, stitched and sewn over and over for longevity. In time these jackets became a symbol of cultural identiy following a series of wars and humility. These jackets are representive of hard work, and to a degree, cultural pride.
