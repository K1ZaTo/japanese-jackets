Hikeshi

Hikeshi (hee-ke-she), literally meaning firemen, soaked their jackets with water before going into an inferno. The jackets provided adequete protection for the brave firemen but were quite heavy. Adorned with somekind of ‘mon’ to distinguish departments, these jackets serve fashion more so than function today.
